How To Tell If A Gucci Bag Is Real?

While the best way to tell if a Gucci bag is real or not is to get it checked by an expert, there are some easy signs to highlight whether your bag is fake or real.

1. Stitching

Paying close attention to the stitching on Gucci bags can help you identify a fake from a real one. The stitching is done by hand on Gucci handbagsÂ so it shouldnâ€™t appear to have been done by a machine. If the lines are too straight, it could be a fake. Fake bags also might (but not always) feature much thicker stitching.

2. Hardware

The hardware on fake bags wonâ€™t be as high quality. One way to really notice the hardware is on the zips. Some zips on Gucci bags might have logo engraving, so look out for that, too.

One of the easiest ways to tell if a Gucci bag is real is to pay attention to an intricate hardware detail. This could be the Dionysus hardware on the front of the bag, as that is a difficult feature to replicate.

3. Logo Detailing

Whether itâ€™s a Gucci logo plaque or engraved logo lettering, this can be a good sign of the authenticity of your bag. The logo plaques should be made meticulously, notice if any areas are thinner or chunkier than usual. For logo engravings, make sure that the spacing is correct and that it is properly placed.

The same can be said for the box and dust bag that the bag originally came in. Check for small details in the logo print.

4. Interior Logo Tab

The front of the leather tag inside the bag should have three lines of text made by heat stamp:

  2. The middle is the 'Gucci' font
  3. The bottom should say 'made in italy' in lowercase

5. Serial Numbers

Note how the serial numbers are displayed. Inside contemporary Gucci bags, you'll find a leather label inside with a serial number debossed on the underside. The top numbers represent the style code, while the bottom is the supplier code. There should be a total of 10-13 numbers.

Bags produced prior to 1990 will have a different layout of numbers, so make sure to check for the specific era of your bag.
